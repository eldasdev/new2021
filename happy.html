Biz Sizni Yangi Yil bilan tabriklaymiz

Salom hammaga biz Eldas Dasturlash Jamoasi sizni kirib kelayotgan Yangi yil bilan tabriklaymiz. Kelejakdagi ishlaringizga omad tilaymiz. Va hamda Yangi Yilda ham bizning ishlarimizni qo'llab-quvvatlashda davom etasiz degan umiddaman. Xullas, sizni biz chin qalbdan ushbu tantana bilan tabriklayman. Bundan tashqari, sizga keyingi Yangi Yil uchun rejalaringizni tuzib qo'yishingizni maslahat qilaman.

Hi everyone we Eldas Programming Team congratulate you on the coming New Year. We wish you good luck in your future endeavors. And I hope you will continue to support our work in the New Year as well. So, we sincerely congratulate you on this occasion. I also advise you to make your plans for the next New Year.
